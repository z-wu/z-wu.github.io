About Me

I am currently an applied NLP and machine learning scientist working on web-scale open-domain question answering. My general interests are on large scale machine learning using web knowledge, with applications in web search and mining, natural language processing, and social computing. I got my Phd from Computer Science & Engineering at Penn State, advised by Prof. C. Lee Giles. I receieved my B.S. (2008) and M.S. (2011) degree in computer science from Xi'an Jiaotong Univeristy.
